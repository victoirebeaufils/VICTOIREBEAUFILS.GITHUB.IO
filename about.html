Hi! My name is Victoire and I'm a first-year CS student. I grew up in Paris and moved to Indiana to study at Purdue University. I've always been interested in programming but didn't have many opportunities to learn how to code in school. The lack of opportunities made me travel to Boston where I participated in a 2-week Android Studio summer camp back in the summer of 2017. Now, I'm studying Computer Science with the hope of improving areas such as Acessibility, Education and help solve environmental issues.

I've always loved making stuff, and I've found that photography was one of the best mediums to express my ideas. I love making series and photographing the human body. In my free time, I like to take portraits of my friends and always make it a challenge to capture interesting emotions.

This website was made using Bootstrap 4, the flag-icon-css and fontawesome libraries.
