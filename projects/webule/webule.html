About this project

Why I decided to make it

Time management has been something I’ve struggled with a lot these past few months as I tried adapting to the college rhythm. Balancing activities and school was something I never really had to do as my life in France was defined by the steady rhythm of a 4-hour school week with a sprinkle of homework and assignments here and there. In college, I quickly figured I’d be in need of a planner, and I started using Google Calendar as it would sync with my phone and computer. It worked quite well, except for the few times where time zones got in the way. During the few times I travelled in Chicago, because I’d previously chosen to set up the time on my phone manually, my meetings were all scheduled for an hour after they were supposed to start. When I had to plan big trips, it got worse. I was desperately in need of an app that would allow me to store all of my travel information and reservations.
