Coal and the environment


About this project

Just like many other people, I’ve gained awareness of climate change and the consequences for our planet. I often felt powerless seeing just how small of an impact the actions I did seemed to have and decided to turn this feeling into a series of photographs depicting my relationship with our changing planet. I used coal as one of my primary mediums to turn what seems like figures and statistics regarding CO2 levels and pollution into something more tangible and illustrated.
